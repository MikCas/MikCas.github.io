Raytracer

Graphics

A CPU-based path tracer built from the ground up, initially grounded in Peter Shirley's Raytracing in One Weekend. The engine implements ray–object intersections, progressive sampling, depth of field, physically based materials (dielectric, metal, Lambertian), and a modular scene composition system.

The long-term focus is performance: a procedural, data-driven scene system for algorithmically generated environments, and a parallel compute layer to maximise rendering throughput on multi-core hardware.
